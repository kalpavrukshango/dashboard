About Us

Kalpavruksha Rural Development and Educational Sangha is a registered nonprofit organization working in Mundgod, Karnataka since 2021. We aim to empower rural and tribal communities through education, sustainable development, environmental stewardship, and inclusive growth initiatives.

Our Core Areas of Work

Education
Improving access, quality, and inclusion.
Environment
Tree plantations, forest conservation, eco-awareness.
Livelihoods
Sustainable agriculture, SHGs, skill training.
Youth & Sports
Events, training, rural athlete support.
Women Empowerment
SHGs, tailoring, digital literacy.
Tribal Development
Holistic support for indigenous communities.

Key Projects

School Infrastructure Support

Enhanced learning environments by providing clean water, drainage, and furniture to government schools.

Sports & Youth Empowerment

Organized rural-level tournaments, distributed uniforms, and encouraged youth participation in sports.

Vanamahotsava Initiative

Mass tree plantation with the Karnataka Forest Dept and villagers to promote environmental conservation.

Relief Distribution Drive

Provided essential food and relief kits in remote regions like Ankola and Shirur during crisis periods.

Community Office Setup

Maintained a dedicated office space to coordinate and monitor developmental and outreach activities.
