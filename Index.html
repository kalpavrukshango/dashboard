About Kalpavruksha

Kalpavruksha Rural Development and Educational Sangha is a registered nonprofit organization working in Mundgod, Karnataka since 2021. We empower rural and tribal communities through education, sustainable development, environmental stewardship, and inclusive growth initiatives.

Our interventions are community-driven, research-backed, and designed to create long-term positive impact. Inspired by the name “Kalpavruksha”, our organisation seeks to be a source of growth and transformation for society.

Our Core Areas

Education

Improving access, quality and inclusive opportunities for children and adults.

Environment

Tree plantations, forest conservation and eco-awareness programs.

Livelihoods

Skill training, SHGs support, and sustainable agriculture techniques.

Youth & Sports

Local tournaments, coaching and youth engagement activities.

Women Empowerment

Self-help groups, tailoring, micro-entrepreneurship and digital literacy.

Tribal Development

Holistic community support for indigenous groups.

Key Projects

School Infrastructure Support

Improved sanitation, clean water & furniture for government schools to create better learning spaces.

Sports & Youth Empowerment

Organising rural tournaments, distributing kits and motivating young athletes.

Vanamahotsava Initiative

Mass tree plantation drives in partnership with Karnataka Forest Department and local communities.

Relief Distribution Drive

Food and relief kits for remote communities during crises.

Community Office Setup

A dedicated coordination hub for outreach and monitoring.
